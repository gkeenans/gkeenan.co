Look. I'm just an artist trying to make it all work.

If you want to help support me, here's how you can.

Share

First off, something simple and easy you can do is share my work. Maybe it's a photo. Maybe it's a blog post. I dunno, it's just really nice that you like my stuff enough to give it a boost.

Subscribe

Subscribe to my projects via RSS:

  • A Very Good Blog
  • A Very Good (Audio) Blog (RSS / Apple Podcasts / Or wherever you get your podcasts)

I'm also on Mastodon (for some reason) and Glass (to share photos).

Also also there's this cute lil omg.lol page that has a bunch of other links to other places I exist around the web.

Survive

Capitalism is broken. You know it. I know it. We all know it. I would love to be able to fix it. However, I am but a single moron, so I'm just trying to survive it.

If you feel compelled to reward my work with a monetary donation, that's so friggin nice. I could not be more grateful.

Feel free to use this Ko-Fi widget.

My Ko-Fi page is also where I host my Print Shop, so if you would like something more tangible in exchange for your cash, might I suggest a very lovely and unique photo to hang on your wall?
